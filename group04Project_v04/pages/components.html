CSS Component: Modal Pop-up

Modal popups are used pretty frequently on the web. Some popular uses of them include driving newsletter sign ups, displaying notifications/alerts, and handling register and login forms.

Our modal will be generic which means you will be free to use it for whatever purpose you'd like. The modal pop-up will be embedded in a typed link for users to click.
